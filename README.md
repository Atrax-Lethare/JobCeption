## Purpose
Define JobCeption's vision, the problem it solves, the users it serves, and the metrics by which success will be measured. This is the highest-level product spec; every functional decision should trace back to something defined here.

## Vision
A hiring market where reputation, demonstrated skill, and accountability matter more than keywords, referrals, and politics.

## Problem
Hiring is broken on both sides. Candidates pour hours into applications that vanish into ATS systems, get ghosted after final-round interviews, and never receive feedback. Recruiters drown in low-quality applications, lose top candidates to slow internal cycles, and watch offers collapse to counteroffers and no-shows. Twenty specific failure modes are catalogued in the project specification document.

## Solution
A dual-sided platform built around four interlocking systems:
1. **Bounties** — skill-based challenges that double as job applications, freelance gigs, or XP events.
2. **Progress Indicator** — a transparent, mutually accountable hiring pipeline tracker.
3. **Connections** — a professional social layer with badges and creative XP.
4. **Dynamic Profile** — a living portfolio replacing the static résumé.

All four systems are bound together by a **Reputation** economy that punishes bad behavior on both sides.

## Target Users
- **Candidates:** students, early-career professionals, mid-career switchers, and senior practitioners willing to prove skill through work.
- **Recruiters & Companies:** in-house recruiters, startups, hiring managers, and agencies tired of spam-volume hiring.
- **Freelance Clients:** organizations posting one-off project bounties with cash rewards.

## Guiding Principles
- Prove, don't promise.
- Both sides are accountable.
- Time is sacred — every stage is time-boxed.
- Transparency by default — salary, requirements, timelines visible upfront.
- Feedback is the product.
- One commitment, one reputation.

## Success Metrics
- **Time-to-Hire** — median days from bounty publish to pledged offer.
- **Pledge Honor Rate** — percentage of pledges (both sides) honored. Target ≥ 95%.
- **Ghost Rate** — percentage of pipelines abandoned silently. Target ≤ 2%.
- **Feedback Coverage** — percentage of candidates receiving structured feedback. Target 100%.
- **Salary Honesty** — median delta between declared range and final offer. Target ≤ 5%.
- **Bounty Completion Rate** — submissions per bounty join.
- **Candidate NPS** and **Recruiter NPS**.

## Out of Scope (MVP)
- Native mobile apps (web-only at launch).
- Multi-language UI (English-first).
- Public REST API for third-party integrations.
- White-label deployments.

## Open Questions
- Vertical-first (engineering only?) or horizontal launch?
- Free for candidates and paid for recruiters, or freemium both sides?
- Geographic launch market — India-first or global?
