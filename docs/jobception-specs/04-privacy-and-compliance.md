## Purpose
Capture privacy and compliance obligations across jurisdictions JobCeption operates in.

## Applicable Regimes
- **DPDP Act 2023** (India) — primary launch jurisdiction.
- **GDPR** (EU/EEA) — if any user is in scope.
- **CCPA/CPRA** (California) — if any user is in scope.

## User Rights
- Access (data export within 30 days).
- Rectification.
- Erasure (subject to legal holds — pledges retained per `02-data-governance.md`).
- Portability (machine-readable export).
- Object/restrict processing.

## Consent
- Explicit opt-in for marketing emails.
- Granular cookie consent on first visit.
- Re-consent on material changes to processing.

## Data Subject Requests (DSR)
- Self-serve export and deletion in Settings.
- 30-day SLA on assisted requests.
- Identity verification required before fulfilling.

## Children
- Minimum age 16. Sign-ups under 16 rejected. KYC double-checks.

## Cross-Border Transfers
- Standard contractual clauses for transfers out of EU.
- India-launch data stays in India.

## DPIA
- Required before launching any new high-risk feature (e.g., webcam Anti-Cheat).

## Vendor Compliance
- All processors bound by DPA.
- Subprocessor list published and updated quarterly.

## Cookies
- Strictly necessary: session, CSRF.
- Functional: language, theme.
- Analytics: opt-in.
